Create and Share Your Projects With Fellow Creatives at UIUC!

FimR is now open for popular consumption at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Collaborate and Stay on Track with you Projects' Tasks

FimR give users a task manager so that you can keep track of what's need to make your project a success!

Create

Create public or private projects, on your own or with a team. Add details, positions you are looking to fill, and a description to let others know what your project is all about.

Collaborate

Public projects will be featured on the Discover page. Members will be able to look through all projects and show their interest in helping you. On FimR you can stay in touch with collaborators after you have finished your project.

Stay On Track

Once you have your project team, you will get a dedicated task manager to help you keep track of your project's progress. FimR is here to help you along the way so you can successfully start and finish your project.
